Tomatillo Sauce

A simple roasted tomatillo sauce. It can be eaten on it's own with chips or used as an excellent verde enchillade sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb tomatillos
  • Hot Peppers (1 - 2 Jalapenos or 3 - 4 Serrano)
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 -5 Garlic Clove
  • 1 1/2 Cups Chicken Stock
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Take all the vegetables and put them on a rimmed baking sheet. Place under a preheated broiler. Once softend, blotchy, and blackening flip and roast the other side.
  2. Cool and then transfer to a blender, make sure to include the juices from the tomatillos. Blend into a course puree.
  3. Preheat oil in a large skillit and then fry puree untill thickened, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken stock and let rethicken.
  5. Use immediately, or store in fridge for up to 2 weeks
